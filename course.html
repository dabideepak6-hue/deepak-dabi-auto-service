⚡ Chapter 1: Electric Vehicle Basics

An electric vehicle uses electrical energy stored in a battery to drive an electric motor. Unlike petrol and diesel vehicles, EVs do not require fuel combustion engines.

The main parts of an EV are:

  • High Voltage Battery Pack
  • Battery Management System (BMS)
  • Electric Motor
  • Motor Controller
  • DC-DC Converter
  • Charger
  • Vehicle Control Unit (VCU)
  • Display System
  • Wiring Harness

A professional EV technician must understand how every component works together. Any fault in battery, controller, motor or communication system can stop the complete vehicle.

🔋 Chapter 2: EV Battery Repair Training

Battery is the heart of an electric vehicle. Most modern EVs use Lithium Ion battery technology because it provides high energy density, long life and better performance.

In this module you will learn:

  • Lithium Ion Cell Types
  • Battery Pack Construction
  • Series and Parallel Connection
  • Voltage Measurement
  • Internal Resistance Testing
  • Cell Balancing
  • Battery Charging Problems
  • Battery Safety

You will understand how to identify weak cells, replace damaged cells and repair battery packs professionally.

⚡ Chapter 3: Battery Management System (BMS) Training

BMS Electric Vehicle battery का सबसे important electronic control system होता है। यह battery की safety, charging, discharging और cell balancing को control करता है।

एक professional EV technician के लिए BMS की complete knowledge होना बहुत जरूरी है क्योंकि अधिकतर battery related problems BMS fault के कारण आती हैं।

आप इस Module में सीखेंगे:

  • BMS का Working Principle
  • Cell Voltage Monitoring
  • Temperature Sensor Checking
  • Over Voltage Protection
  • Under Voltage Protection
  • Over Current Protection
  • Short Circuit Protection
  • Active और Passive Balancing
  • BMS Communication
  • CAN Bus System
  • BMS Fault Diagnosis

BMS खराब होने पर vehicle में charging problem, range कम होना, power cut, error code और battery shutdown जैसी problems आ सकती हैं।

इस course में आप multimeter, diagnostic tools और practical testing methods से BMS fault finding सीखेंगे।

🖥 Chapter 5: EV Controller Repair Training

Controller EV vehicle का brain होता है। यह battery से आने वाली power को motor तक control करके भेजता है।

Controller में MOSFET, MCU, capacitors, resistors और communication circuits होते हैं।

Controller Module में सीखेंगे:

  • Controller Working Principle
  • 3 Phase Motor Control
  • MOSFET Testing
  • Short Circuit Checking
  • MCU Function
  • Throttle Signal Testing
  • Brake Cut Off System
  • Error Code Diagnosis
  • Controller Programming
  • Controller Replacement Process

Controller fault होने पर vehicle start नहीं होना, jerking, low power और motor not responding जैसी problems आती हैं।

इस module में real fault finding techniques और testing procedures सीखेंगे।
