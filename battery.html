Battery Parts

Battery Pack

Battery Pack is the main power source of an electric vehicle.

Lithium Cells

18650, 21700 and Prismatic Cells are commonly used in EVs.

BMS

Battery Management System protects the battery from overcharge, over-discharge and short circuit.

Fuse

HV Fuse protects the battery during fault conditions.

Battery Inspection Checklist

  • ✅ Battery Voltage Check
  • ✅ Cell Voltage Check
  • ✅ Cell Balancing
  • ✅ Temperature Check
  • ✅ Connector Inspection
  • ✅ Fuse Inspection
  • ✅ Charging Test
  • ✅ BMS Communication

Battery Faults

Battery Not Charging

Possible Causes • Charger Fault • BMS Fault • Fuse Open • Loose Connector

Battery Heating

Possible Causes • Cell Damage • Overload • Fan Failure • High Temperature

Low Range

Possible Causes • Weak Cell • Low SOC • Cell Imbalance • Battery Aging

Battery Repair Procedure

  1. Vehicle Power OFF
  2. Remove Battery Safely
  3. Wear HV Gloves
  4. Measure Battery Voltage
  5. Measure Individual Cell Voltage
  6. Check BMS
  7. Check Fuse
  8. Repair or Replace Faulty Parts
  9. Reassemble Battery Pack
  10. Charge Battery
  11. Road Test

Safety Rules

  • ⚠ Never Short Circuit Battery.
  • ⚠ Always Wear PPE.
  • ⚠ Never Open Live Battery.
  • ⚠ Use Insulated Tools.
  • ⚠ Disconnect Charger Before Repair.
